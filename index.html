☀ My Take on Miami ☀

A Guide to Off-Campus Activities at the University of Miami

The University of Miami is situated in the beautiful city of Coral Gables - located in Miami. Students studying at the University of Miami have the luxury of enjoying a big city while being separated from the constant hustle and bustle.

Photo courtesy of Wikimedia Commons

When students aren’t studying or doing homework in Richter Library, there are many things to do in the surrounding area. This website explores -- in my humble opinion -- the best daytime activities, places to eat, nightlife, and special events in Miami.
